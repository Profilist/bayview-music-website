Fall/Spring Showcase

Wednesday, November 29, 2023

Richmond Hill Centre for Performing Arts

The Bayview Music Department hosts showcases of all their ensembles alongside various side performers twice a year. These events are performed inside the Richmond Hill Centre for Performing Arts.

Coffeehouse

Thursday, December 14, 2023

Bayview Secondary School

Every year at Bayview, two Coffeehouse events take place where numerous performers, whether it be musicians or dance groups, perform within a cafe-like environment. During this time, everyone is offered refreshments such as pie and hot chocolate.

The final music performance of the school year held in June. Ensembles and side performers showcase their skills culminated after a year. The past year, Finale was held in the back parking lot of Bayview SS, with free admission and purchasable refreshments like pizza.
